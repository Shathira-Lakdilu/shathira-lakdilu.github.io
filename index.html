About Me

PCB Design Engineer with 2+ years of experience designing and testing multi-layer boards (4-10 layers) for medical technology, wearable devices, and embedded systems. I specialize in ECG/EMG acquisition systems, high-speed DDR4/LPDDR4 routing, RF antenna design, and power electronics.

Core Competencies

🔬 Medical Devices

ECG/EMG front-ends (TI ADS129x series), low-noise analog layout, wearable sensors

⚡ High-Speed Design

DDR4/LPDDR4 routing, signal integrity, transmission line design, impedance control

📡 RF & Wireless

2.4GHz antenna design, impedance matching, NanoVNA validation, S-parameters

🔋 Power Electronics

BMS design, USB-PD 3.0, high-current routing, thermal management
